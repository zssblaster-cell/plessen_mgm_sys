Connect Scanner

Scan for USB, Bluetooth, and WiFi scanners. USB HID scanners (Zebra, Honeywell, Datalogic) work plug-and-play — just click the field and scan. Bluetooth scanners need to be paired via your OS settings first.

Click a button above to discover devices, or plug in USB scanner and click below.
WiFi scanners require the plessen_scanner_bridge.js script running on one office computer. All other devices on the network can then use it. USB HID scanners work without any bridge.
Camera scanning: Click the Camera button in the scanner bar below the action tabs. Works on phones (rear camera) and desktops with a webcam — no app install needed.
